Bullet Train

The movie "Bullet Train" (2022) is an action thriller based on the novel "Maria Beetle" by Kotaro Isaka. The film features an ensemble cast, including Brad Pitt, Joey King, Aaron Taylor-Johnson, Brian Tyree Henry, and others. The story revolves around a group of assassins with conflicting missions who find themselves on the same train. Each assassin is unaware of the others' presence, leading to a high-stakes and intense situation as their paths intersect during the journey. The narrative unfolds with a mix of action, suspense, and unexpected twists.

Die Hard

"Die Hard" is a classic action film directed by John McTiernan and was released in 1988. The movie stars Bruce Willis as the main character, John McClane. A New York City police detective, travels to Los Angeles to reconcile with his estranged wife, Holly Gennaro (Bonnie Bedelia), who is attending a Christmas party at Nakatomi Plaza, the high-rise office building where she works. The festive atmosphere takes a dark turn when a group of terrorists led by Hans Gruber (Alan Rickman) seize control of the building.

Tom Clancy's Jack Ryan

"Tom Clancy's Jack Ryan" is a television series that premiered on Amazon Prime Video. It is based on the fictional character Jack Ryan, a CIA analyst created by author Tom Clancy. The series stars John Krasinski as Jack Ryan and follows his adventures in various high-stakes situations involving terrorism, geopolitics, and national security. Jack Ryan, initially a desk-bound analyst, is thrust into the field as he uncovers a series of suspicious bank transfers that draw him into a world of international terrorism. As Ryan investigates, he discovers a growing global threat orchestrated by powerful adversaries.

Attack on Titan

"Attack on Titan" is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. The series has also been adapted into an anime, which gained international acclaim. The story is set in a world where humanity resides within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from the Titans, gigantic humanoid creatures that devour humans seemingly without reason. The narrative follows Eren Yeager, his adoptive sister Mikasa Ackerman, and their friend Armin Arlert, who join the military to fight the Titans after their hometown is devastated and Eren's mother is killed by one of the Titans.
